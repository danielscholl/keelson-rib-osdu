## Where this rib fits

The rib surfaces CIMPL **platform delivery and operations**: cluster health
and Flux topology, release quality, epic and MR activity, CVE remediation,
and your personal review queue. The OSDU **data plane** (storage records,
schemas, search, entitlements, legal tags) is deliberately out of scope; that
surface is reached through direct OSDU platform API access, not through this
rib, so the rib stays a thin, sidecar-free view over delivery and ops.
